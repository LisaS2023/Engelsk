India – A Big Country

India is a very big country in Asia. More than one billion people live there. The capital is New Delhi. Mumbai is the biggest city.

India has many languages. Hindi is the main language, but many people also speak English. India has old traditions and many religions like Hinduism and Buddhism.

Indian food is famous around the world. People eat rice, bread, and curry. India also makes many films. The film industry in Mumbai is called Bollywood.

India became free from Britain in 1947. A man called Mahatma Gandhi helped India become free. He did this without violence. Today, India has both very rich and very poor people.

04 · Reading

An Introduction to India

The Republic of India is one of the most populous countries in the world. The capital is New Delhi, but Mumbai is the largest city. India is a democracy with 28 states. Since 1991, economic reforms have made India one of the fastest growing economies. However, many people still live in poverty.

A Country of Diversity
Four major religions started in India: Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism. Hindi is the official language, but English is also widely used. In total, 23 languages are spoken.

A Rich History
India has always been known for its trade and culture. From the 1500s, European countries built trading posts. By the 1800s, most of India was controlled by the British East India Company.

Independence
India became independent in 1947 after non-violent protests led by Mahatma Gandhi. Because of religious tensions, Pakistan and Bangladesh were created.

Modern India
India has experienced a technological boom. The country has a large high-tech economy and a strong workforce. However, there is still a huge contrast between rich and poor.
